What is Daily Rating?

Daily Rating is a simple app that allows you to rate your day on a scale of 1 to 5 stars. It's designed to help you reflect on your day and track your mood over time. Whether you're having a great day or a tough one, Daily Rating provides a quick and easy way to capture how you're feeling.

More than you think

We further divide how you feel about your day into two aspects: Activation and Valence. These two dimensions are known by Russell's Circumplex Model of Affect, a widely accepted framework in psychology for understanding emotions. Activation measures how energized or lethargic you feel, while Valence captures how positive or negative your emotions are. By tracking both Activation and Valence, Daily Rating gives you a more nuanced understanding of your daily experiences.

Just for your eyes

Any data you input is stored locally on your device unless you choose to back it up to the cloud. We take your privacy seriously and ensure that your data is secure and only accessible to you. We achieve this by encrypting your data and using keys that only you have access to. This way, you can feel confident that your personal information is safe with us.
